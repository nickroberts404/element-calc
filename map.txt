Example
Files: 
	app.js
	element_list.js
	util.js
	/components
		table.js
		element.js
		data_view.js

app.js will depend on table and data. It will have click handlers for elements, and will maintain a list of selected elements.

data.js will display the molecular mass, and formuala.

util.js will have utility methods to handle the math.

table.js will render our periodic table.

element will be the actual element, will trigger a click handler, and will display the abbr., mass, and atomic number.
